How to Play 🎴

  • Cards are shown face-up for 3 seconds — memorize them!
  • Click any card to flip it over.
  • Flip a second card to find its match.
  • One English card + one Japanese card = a match!
  • No match? Both cards flip back — try again!
  • Use hints to briefly reveal all unmatched cards.
  • Match consecutively to build combo and bonus points!
  • Fewer mistakes = more stars at the end.
  • 🏆 Match all pairs to win!
